Lalisa Manobal

(also spelled Manoban; born Pranpriya Manobal; March 27, 1997), known mononymously as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer and dancer.

Jennie Kim

(Korean: 김제니; born January 16, 1996), known mononymously as Jennie, is a South Korean singer and rapper. Born and raised in South Korea, Jennie studied in New Zealand for five years before returning to South Korea in 2010.

Roseanne Park

Roseanne Park (born 11 February 1997), known mononymously as Rosé (Korean: 로제) , is a Korean-New Zealand singer and dancer based in South Korea. Born in New Zealand and raised in Australia.

Kim Ji-soo

Kim Ji-soo (Korean: 김지수; born January 3, 1995), known mononymously as Jisoo, is a South Korean singer and actress.
